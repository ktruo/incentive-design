FAQ

Addressing the two big fears.

"I don't want to help patients switch to competitors."

Points ensure reciprocity. Clinics can only read if they also contribute, and authorship is redacted to minimize competitive targeting.

"What if another physio judges my treatment?"

Standardized forms reduce subjective commentary. Optional details cost more points and are behind additional consent.

Do clinics see each other's honor ratings?

No. Honor ratings stay private and are only visible inside your own clinic dashboard.
