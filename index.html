Problem

Brief and direct.

Patient history is fragmented across clinics. Most clinics want shared history, few want to share theirs — creating misaligned incentives that keep patients starting over.

Anonymity & Judgement Reduction

Driven by the example artefacts above.

No clinic identity is exposed.

Each document is source-blind. Clinics never see who authored a record, which keeps sharing neutral and low-risk.

Summaries and forms reduce interpretation.

AI summaries abstract documentation styles, and structured fields prevent judgement about how another clinic writes notes.

Silent Quality Incentives

Outcome only, not the system.

Thorough histories earn faster access.

Clinics that consistently share detailed histories earn points faster and spend fewer points over time.

Low-effort histories cost more.

Clinics that share low-effort histories experience higher point costs, without any visible ratings or labels.
