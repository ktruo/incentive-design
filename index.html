Mechanics

Credit reciprocity

Reading costs credits. Publishing earns credits and restores access.

Shared match pool

Part of read spend goes to a pool paid to contributors each round.

Privacy-first

Authorship is redacted to avoid reputational fear.

Quality enforcement

Low-quality entries can trigger slashing and reputation loss.

Prototype is a simplified behavioral model for incentive design testing.
